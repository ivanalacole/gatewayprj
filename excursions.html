Things To Do In Turks & Caicos

Jet Ski

Have an amaazing time on our clear water beaches on a jet ski. This excursion will last you about 1 hour of fun.

Snorkeling

Snorkeling is the practice of swimming on or through a body of water while equipped with a diving mask, a shaped breathing tube called a snorkel, and usually swimfins. It is such a fun activity.

Parasailing

Parasailing is a recreational kiting activity where a person is towed behind a Fury boat while attached to a specially designed parachute, known as a parasail wing.

Sight Seeing

Turks and Caicos has various murals, landmarks, as well as beaches that will catch your eye.

Horseback Riding

A well-run, well-executed adventure off the beaten path of Turks and through the aquamarine waters in the saddle of a beautiful horse.

Clear Kyak Tour

Embark on a fun-filled and adventurous guided crystal kayak tour. Explore the mangrove and the amazing marine creatures that call it home and continue to an island with iguanas to learn about the endemic rock iguanas, crabs and birds.
